### How to install stadium :

1. Make sure you have already installed **stadium server** version 1.00 and 1.20 and 1.25 and 1.30 and 1.40 and 1.50 and 1.55 (complete Download [HERE](https://instructions.pesmodding.com/2021/06/how-to-properly-install-stadium-server.html)).
   And then download the latest version 1.60 [HERE](https://modsfire.com/kdz1obU1bTBcguP)
2. Unpack the v1.60 archive and copy folders modules and content into your sider folder (overwrite when asked)
3. Add in sider.config in modules section **lua.module = "StadiumServer.lua"**
4. Go to sider directory and open content/stadium-server/map_competitions
5. Configure your map competitions for every Stadium:
   **65535, Stadium ID (usually is 009), Stadium Name, Directory where the Stadium is (like Serie A/San Paolo), , , # Team Name**
6. Open content/stadium-server/map_teams
7. Configure your map teams: Copy the map competitions string and substitute 65535 with the team ID

### FAQ

**Where can I find Team ID?** <br />
Search a team on PesMaster.com and observe the link: https://www.pesmaster.com/napoli/pes-2019/team/327/
In this case the Team ID is 327.

**Where can I find Stadium ID?** <br />
Go to common/render/thumbnail/stadium and the ID is that written in Image Name, for example st009, 009 is the ID.
