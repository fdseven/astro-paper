  ## Present to you the long-awaited UEFA Champions League scoreboard. originally made by @spursfan18, remastered by @papijonnn, using his as a base and improving a few textures, and overall positioning to make it literally spot on to the real-life scoreboard.​
  <hr />

The previous PES 2021 Champions League scoreboard had the time seemingly bunched into a small box, Papijonn has expanded that area of the scoreboard to match up with it's real-life counterpart. the original scoreboard was also seemingly low on the screen, he corrected this and moved it to the correct position based on real images of course.​

Feature included the official font with some slight tweaks to the original released previously. it is optional but HIGHLY recommend you use the font as it brings realism to the scoreboard.​

### How to install

Installation is simple, just add the files to your scoreboard-server and add the font to your "livecpk" folder and add the line <br />
`"cpk.root = ".\livecpk\papijon_font""` <br />
to your sider.ini. Finally add the lines from the invluded "ad to your map.txt" file
