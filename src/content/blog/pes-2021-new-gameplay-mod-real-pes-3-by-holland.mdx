  ## The new gameplay mod from Holland called "Real PES 3" for PES 2021. Is a different gameplay from previous versions because it differs from greater freedom on the pitch and greater mastery of our players pad in hand.
  
  <hr />

### Changelog

IT'S ESSENTIAL TO DELETE ALL THE FILES OF OTHER GAMEPLAY MODS TO FULLY SEE THE IMPLEMENTED NEWS, DON'T FORGET THAT IT IS VERY IMPORTANT!!!

KNOW THAT THOSE WHO DO NOT HAVE A MODIFIED EDIT/DB..........BUT I IMAGINE YOU ALL DO, MAY NOT SEE THE SAME RESPONSES WE ARE SEEING!!!

REAL PES 3 is a different gameplay from previous versions because it differs from greater freedom on the pitch and greater mastery of our players pad in hand.

You will find a modified exe where I bypassed an address with codes not executed in the exe to make the cpu more "normal"
for those who cannot use the Steam version, they will have to install the cpk version in the appropriate folder.

In "data" you will find the dt18 that I did some time ago where I implemented both fouls and penalties, but also greater shooting power ONLY in penalty kicks making them less difficult to score.

In the path "livecpk\REAL PES 3\common\script\flow" you will find some folders containing a json file:
"REAL PES 3"

In the file I worked on the CPU_LEVEL by setting a high value only for the Legend level (85), this is to show you the clear difference compared to vanilla.
The more you raise the value, the less challenge you will notice (85 is very high), on the contrary, the more you decrease it, the greater the CPU challenge will be.
I set it this high to make the challenge easy for you, but obviously you have to decrease it yourself until you find your perfect balance.
REMEMBER.............DECIMAL VALUES ONLY!!!

I think that "15" in value could be fine, but it is always a subjective matter.
Same thing for the other values, the last one is Legend and the penultimate one is Superstar..............and so on.
