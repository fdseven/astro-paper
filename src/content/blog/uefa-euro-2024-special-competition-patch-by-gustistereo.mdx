### From GustiStereo

Hello. I'm currently working to try and update the UEFA Euro from 2020 to 2024

So far I have replaced/made/customized the following items:

- Ball Adidas FUSSBALLLIEBE (Credits to Hawke)
- UEFA Euro 2024 logo (emblems in 256 and 512 - white and dark version) (credits to https://peslogos.blogspot.com)
- Database of the Competitions: updated the name of the competition to "UEFA Euro 2024" and modified the allowed substitutions from 3 to 5
- Uniforms for the children (jersey_child). Originals for Euro 2020 were in red, I made these ones in blue with the Euro 2024 logo (However, I never saw them in the competition)
- Background for the competition
- Substitute board
- Tunnel arch
- Video Intros (made with video presentations of the competition I found on Internet)
- Badges
- Scoreboard, based on the theme used in the UEFA.tv site for the Euro and the UEFA Euro mobile App.

### How to Install

GustiStereo have decided to make the Mod for sider and put the updated database (CompetitionRegulation.bin, to replace the competition name and modify the allowed substitutions) in a separate pack as an Add-On, since it may be in conflict with some patches causing the game to crash.

Copy the content of the folder "Sider" (or Sider-FL for the Football Life version) into your Sider folder.
It includes 2 (two) subfolders:

- content
- livecpk

Then, add the following line to sider.ini to enable livecpk folder for the Euro

{" "}

<pre>
  <code>
    <span class="text-white">cpk.root = ".\livecpk\Euro"</span>
  </code>
</pre>

The "content" folder includes the badges for the badge-server. You have to replace the content inside the "Euro" folder. The map.txt and map_team.txt are the default ones since those already have the lines for the UEFA Euro and the BOH (Badge of Honor) for Italy.

The Sider version for Football Life also includes the folders that should be replaced into the `/content/omb` folder (which is the ball-server on Football Life).

The latest version of the Ball-Server from Hawke already includes the Adidas FUSSBALLLIEBE, so you shouldn't do anything about it.
In the case you are not using any ball server, then the official match ball for the UEFA Euro 2024 is allready included into the livecpk folder, so it will replace the Adidas Uniforia from Euro 2020.
