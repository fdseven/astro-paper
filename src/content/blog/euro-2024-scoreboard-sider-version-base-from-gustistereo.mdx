  ## Hello guys, I converted Euro 2024 scoreboard from GustiStereo Euro 2024 patch to sider version. 
  
  <hr />

### Thanks to GustiStereo for amazingly Euro 2024 patch and it's database

You can download the patch [HERE](https://www.pesmania.web.id/posts/pes-2021-uefa-euro-2024-special-competition-patch-gustistereo).

From that pach, the Euro 2024 scoreboard automatically included independently for Euro 2024 Competition. But there's no selection or randomly of TV channels, so I converted them to sider version and enhance some graphic with some TV channels and pause menu graphic and also the replay wipe.

The TV channels I provided are :

- RCTI
- Bein Sports
- BBC Sports
- ESPN Sports
- Sky Sports
- UEFA.tv
